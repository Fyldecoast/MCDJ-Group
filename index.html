About Us

A Family Business Built on Trust & Quality

We’re the Mkala family. Between the four of us, we’ve combined our backgrounds in hospitality and operations to build a property business that actually listens.

We look after short term rentals and serviced stays. The way we work is simple: we lease your property long-term, becoming your single, reliable tenant. From there, we handle the guests, the cleaning, and the maintenance all while making sure your rent is paid on time, every time.

Coming from a boutique hotel background, we know that the small details matter. We don't just 'manage' a house; we keep it in showroom condition. For us, it’s about building a reputation for reliability so you never have to worry about the state of your investment.

Our Mission

To give property owners total peace of mind. We take the stress out of being a landlord by providing guaranteed rent and keeping every home in showroom condition, so you can enjoy the rewards of your investment without the daily headaches.

Our Vision

Our goal is to be the most reliable name in local property management. As a family, we’re committed to growing a business built on long-term relationships, high standards, and the simple promise that when we look after a property, we look after it for life.

The Process

How Rent-to-Rent Works

A simple, transparent partnership model designed to benefit property owners with guaranteed income and professional management.

01

Property Review

We assess your property to ensure it meets our quality standards and is suitable for short-term rentals or serviced accommodation.

02

Agreement & Setup

We establish a clear rent-to-rent agreement with guaranteed monthly payments and transparent terms for both parties.

03

Styling & Preparation

Our team prepares the property to guest-ready standards, including furnishing, decoration, and professional photography.

04

Listing & Operations

We list your property on major platforms like Airbnb and Booking.com, handling all bookings, guest communication, and check-ins.

05

Ongoing Management

We provide continuous property management including cleaning, maintenance, inspections, and regular landlord updates.
